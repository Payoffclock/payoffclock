About PayoffClock

PayoffClock was built to answer a simple question in plain numbers: when will I actually be debt-free, and what is waiting costing me?

I built this site after years working in mortgage underwriting, where I spent my days looking at other people's debt, interest rates, and payoff timelines. I kept noticing the same thing — most people have never seen their own numbers laid out clearly. Banks and lenders aren't in the business of showing you the fastest way out of debt; they profit from the interest along the way.

PayoffClock is my attempt to hand that clarity back to you, for free. No account required, no sales pitch — just tools that show your real payoff date, what an extra payment actually saves you, and what waiting is costing you right now.

What You'll Find Here

  • Debt-Free Countdown — your exact payoff date across all your debts
  • Financial Triage — which debt to attack first
  • Mortgage Reality — what you can actually afford, and how to pay it off faster
  • Cost of Waiting — a live counter on what delay costs you
  • Credit Score Impact — what your score is costing you in real dollars
  • Rent vs. Buy — honest math for your specific timeline

A Note on Accuracy

Every tool on this site is built to be as accurate as possible, but results are estimates for informational purposes and don't replace advice from a licensed financial professional. See our Terms of Use for details.
